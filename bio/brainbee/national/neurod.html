Lipid Metabolism Disorders

  • Usually autosomal recessive diseases
  • Involve defects in enzymes or transport proteins responsible for breaking down lipids.
  • Toxic accumulation of specific lipids within cells, particularly in the lysosomes of neurons and other supportive cells in the nervous system.
  • Progressive cell dysfunction and death, resulting in severe neurodegenerative symptoms.

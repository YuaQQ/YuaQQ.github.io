Chapter 7 Infant, Child and Adolescent Brain

  • Schizophrenia精神分裂 → occur because connections in brain are formed incorrectly in early life
  • Average brain-weight of newborn baby → 370 grams (or 13 ounces)
  • Average weight of adult brain → 3 pounds with about 86 billion neurons
  • Immediately after birth, growth rate of brain is about 1% per day
  • Fastest-growing brain region → cerebellum
  • Cerebellum → brain region with most neurons
  • Increase in brain volume → due to growth, multiplication, maturation (differentiation), and migration of cells in brain
  • Process of making synapses → synaptogenesis突触发生
  • White matter → white due to myelin髓磷脂-wrapped nerve fibers
  • During early childhood, brain reduces number of synapses → synaptic pruning突触修剪
  • During critical periods of development → inputs from sensory, motor, and emotional aspects of experiences influence brain development
  • Growth of corpus callosum胼胝体 → explain better learning capacity in adolescence
  • Diffusion tensor imaging (DTI)弥散张量成像 → image white matter fiber tracts
  • Human brains continue to develop until age 30
    • Gray matter density increases in left temporal lobe (memory and language) until age 30
  • Plasticity可塑性 → ability of brain to modify itself and adapt to environmental challenges
    • Classified into:
      • Experience-expectant经验期待（可塑性）
        • Integrating environmental stimuli into various developmental patterns
      • Experience-dependent经验依赖（可塑性）
        • Changes occur because of life experiences
        • Develop throughout life
        • Has no critical period for acquisition
      • Two-photon imaging双光子成像 → allow to observe living neurons using a microscope and to track their growth and development in response to various experiences

Chapter 10 The Body in Balance

  • Tendency of organ systems to maintain equilibrium → homeostasis内稳态
    • Involve hypothalamus
  • Melatonin褪黑素 → hormone that influences sleep behaviors
  • Pineal gland松果体 → secretes melatonin into bloodstream at night
    • Light exposure stops melatonin release → promote wakefulness
    • When body prepares to wake up from sleep → cortisol皮质醇 is released, increasing blood sugar levels
  • Brain regions involved in hormone release → neuroendocrine system神经内分泌系统
  • Paraventricular室旁(核) and supraoptic nuclei视上核 of hypothalamus → extend axons into posterior pituitary后垂体 gland
    • Releases vasopressin加压素and oxytocin催产素
      • Vasopressin (also called antidiuretic hormone抗利尿激素) increases water retention in kidneys and constricts blood vessels (vasoconstriction血管收缩)
      • Oxytocin promotes uterine子宫 contractions during labor分娩 and milk release during nursing
    • Adrenal glands肾上腺 → release stress hormones norepinephrine, epinephrine肾上腺素(又写作adrenaline), and cortisol
    • Corticotropin releasing hormone (CRH) 促肾上腺皮质素释放激素 → secreted from hypothalamus and, triggers release of adrenocorticotropic hormone (ACTH) 促肾上腺皮质激素 from anterior pituitary前垂体 gland
    • ACTH travels to adrenal glands → stimulates release of cortisol
    • Tropic hormones促激素 → stimulate activity of endocrine glands (thyroid甲状腺, adrenal cortex肾上腺皮质, testes睾丸, ovaries卵巢, etc.)
    • Growth hormone生长激素 → stimulates growth and development of bones, muscles and soft tissues
    • Prolactin泌乳素 → stimulates milk production by breasts
    • Gonadotropin-releasing hormone (GnRH) 促性腺激素释放激素 from hypothalamus → makes anterior pituitary release:
      • Luteinizing hormone (LH)促黄体激素
      • Follicle stimulating hormone (FSH) 促卵泡激素
    • Leptin瘦素 and ghrelin饥饿素 change eating behavior by regulating food intake and energy balance
    • Ghrelin:
      • Produced by stomach when it is empty
      • Ghrelin activates hunger circuits in hypothalamus → promotes food-seeking behavior
    • Leptin
      • Secreted from fat cells and is released when adipose脂肪 tissue stores are large
      • Suppresses hunger and minimizes desire to eat
    • Sympathetic branch交感支 → activates adrenal medulla肾上腺髓质 to produce epinephrine (adrenaline) → increases heart rate and dilates blood vessels that supply muscles
    • Cortisol → a type of glucocorticoid; increases production and release of sugars from liver
    • Overexposure to glucocorticoids糖皮质激素 can lead to:
      • Muscle atrophy肌肉萎缩
      • Development of hypertension (high blood pressure)高血压
      • Atherosclerosis (hardening of the arteries)动脉粥样硬化 → heightened risk of heart attacks
      • Suppress immune system function
      • Inhibit neuron growth in hippocampus

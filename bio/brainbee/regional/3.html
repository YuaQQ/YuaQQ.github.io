Chapter 3 Movement

  • Joints关节 = sites where two or more bones come together
  • Muscles that bend a joint = flexors屈肌
  • Muscles that straighten the joint = extensors伸肌
  • Muscles that promote the movement are called agonists主动肌
  • Muscles that oppose or inhibit the movement are antagonists拮抗肌.
  • Skeletal muscle is made up muscle cells, called muscle fibers肌纤维
  • Each muscle fiber controlled by one alpha motor neuron α运动神经元 → originates in spinal cord or brain
  • An alpha motor neuron plus all muscle fibers it innervates → motor unit运动单位
  • Motor neurons die → amyotrophic lateral sclerosis肌萎缩性侧索硬化症 (ALS)
  • Reflexes反射 → stereotyped, automatic muscle responses to particular stimuli
    • Depend on neurons in spinal cord
  • Crossed extension reflex交叉伸展反射 → help maintain balance
  • Motor neurons运动神经元 → longest cells in body
  • Neurons communicate with muscles at neuromuscular junctions神经肌接头
  • Gamma motor neurons → keep muscle spindles肌纺锤 taut
  • Golgi tendon organs高尔基腱器官 → detect how much force or tension is applied to muscles
  • Motor cortex运动皮层 → directly control alpha motor neurons in spinal cord
  • Regions that modulate voluntary movements随意运动
    • Basal ganglia基底神经节, thalamus, cerebellum, midbrain and brainstem
  • In Parkinson’s disease帕金森病, neurons in substantia nigra黑质 die
    • These neurons relay signals to basal ganglia using dopamine多巴胺 → involved in motor control
    • Symptoms of Parkinson’s: resting tremor静止性震颤, rigidity, and akinesia失运动能 = inability to move
  • Huntington’s disease亨廷顿舞蹈病 → lead to uncontrolled jerking or twitching movements
    • Due to loss of inhibitory GABAergic neurons in basal ganglia
  • Cerebellum:
    • Coordinates and fine-tunes skilled movement
    • Ensures proper timing
    • Motor learning and coordination
    • Mediates skillful movements
  • Disturbance of cerebellar function leads to:
    • Poor coordination
    • Problems with balance
    • Difficulties in speech production
  • Long-term alcohol abuse酒精滥用 → cause acquired cerebellar degeneration
    • Symptoms:
      • Poor coordination
      • Unsteady gait步态
      • Changes in speech
      • Difficulty with fine motor skills

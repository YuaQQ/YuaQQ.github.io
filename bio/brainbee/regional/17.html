Chapter 17 Solving Human Problems

  • Deep brain stimulation (DBS)深脑刺激
    • DBS has been used to treat epilepsy癫痫, dystonia肌张力障碍, Tourette’s syndrome妥瑞氏综合症 and obsessive-compulsive disorder强迫症
  • Transcranial magnetic stimulation (TMS) 经颅磁刺激, transcranial direct current stimulation (tDCS) 经颅直流电刺激, and transcranial alternating current stimulation (tACS) 经颅交流电刺激 → all use magnetic fields or low electrical currents to alter neuronal activity in certain parts of cerebral cortex and deeper brain structures
  • Nerve growth factor (NGF)神经生长因子 → slows destruction of cholinergic neurons胆碱能神经元
    • Help retard cognitive decline in Alzheimer’s disease阿尔兹海默症
  • Brain-derived neurotrophic factor (BDNF) 脑源性神经营养因子 → potential for treating Alzheimer’s disease, Huntington’s亨廷顿舞蹈病, Parkinson’s帕金森症, ALS肌萎缩侧索硬化症, and Rett syndrome雷特综合症
  • Adeno-associated virus腺相关病毒 and lentivirus慢病毒 → safest and most efficient vectors for gene therapy
  • CRISPR (Clustered Regularly Interspaced Short Palindromic Repeats) → uses RNA-guided enzymes to snip out or add DNA segments, creating precise alterations in genome
